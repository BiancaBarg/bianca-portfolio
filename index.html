Hi, I’m Bianca, a 3D Character Animator and Animation Mentor graduate from the class of 2025.

I started studying animation in 2023 after discovering my passion for character performance, storytelling, and creating believable emotions through movement.

Before animation, I worked as a mobile app UI designer, and before that as a dental assistant — two very different experiences that shaped my eye for detail, design, patience, and human connection.

I love progress, growth, and constantly evolving. Animation is the perfect place to keep learning, improving, and bringing characters to life one shot at a time.
